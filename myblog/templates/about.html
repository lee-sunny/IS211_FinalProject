About Page

This blog web application was created by Sunah Lee as the final project for IS211 class in Fall of 2019. Hope you enjoy all features.


Features

Navigation

My Blog / Home - Gives you a list of all blog posts from all users in reverse chronologic order.

Login / Register - Allows you to register a username, email and password so that you can gain access to your Dashboard.

Dashboard - Once logged in, you are taken to your own dashboard where you see a list of your posts only (in reverse chronologic order). You can click into individual post titles to update and delete them.
