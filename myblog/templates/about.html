About Page

This blog web application was created by Sunah Lee as the final project for IS211 class in Fall of 2019. Hope you enjoy all features.


Features

Navigation

My Blog / Home - Welcome! The Home page gives you a list of all blog posts from all users in reverse chronologic order.

Register - You can read all posts but cannot create your own posts until you register. You will need to input your username, email, and password.

My Dashboard - Once logged in, you are taken to your own dashboard where you see a list of your posts only (in reverse chronologic order). You can click into individual post titles to update and delete them.

New Post - This link allows you to create a new post. A title and content is required in order to post.

Account - You can update your account in this section. Your username and email can be changed. Once changed, your previous info is deleted. You can also upload a new profile picture.

Logout - After you logout, you can still read posts but cannot edit or delete your posts.
